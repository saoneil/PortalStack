Privacy Policy

This portal is provided for authorized client and club users. We collect only information needed to operate account access and service functionality.

  • Account data: client identifier, username, encrypted password, and session details.
  • Security and audit data: login, logout, and page interaction logs including IP address.
  • Operational use: fraud prevention, account protection, troubleshooting, and service improvements.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information through this portal unless required for account operation. Access is restricted to authorized users and administrators.

If you believe your account data is incorrect or need assistance, please contact your club administrator.
